AirTag Card: Ultra-Thin Wallet Tracker

Disassembled an AirTag and reengineered its power system, main board, and antenna into a compact credit card-sized form factor. This modification allows for discreet wallet tracking without adding bulk, making it an ideal solution for seamless everyday carry.



Ender 3 Upgrades & Optimization

Upgraded and optimized an Ender 3 Pro for improved performance, precision, and reliability. Enhancements include a dual Z-axis for increased stability, a silent control board for quieter operation, an all-metal extruder, an upgraded hotend, and a custom cooling system for better heat management. Additional improvements include automatic bed leveling and custom firmware for enhanced functionality.
